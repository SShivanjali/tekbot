🛠️ CMS & eCommerce

A CMS (Content Management System) is web software used to create and manage website content without needing to modify code.

CMS platforms allow content/media updates through an admin interface.

Popular CMS examples:

WordPress – the most widely used open-source CMS with 80–90% market share for content-based websites.

🛒 eCommerce Platforms

eCommerce platforms help set up an online store to sell products.

Popular platforms include:

Shopify

WooCommerce (built on WordPress)

Magento

PrestaShop

CMS and eCommerce solutions can also be custom-built when needed.
